1. Count Balanced Binary Trees of Height h
	Since the trees should be balanced, the difference between the left and right subtrees cannot
	be more than 1
	count(h) = count(h-1)*count(h-2) +
		   count(h-2)*count(h-1) + 
		   count(h-1)*count(h-1)
